FAQ's

This project focuses on developing various models to classify rice crops based on their salinity tolerance. By leveraging deep learning techniques, we aim to improve crop monitoring and optimize agricultural practices, ensuring better management of salinity stress in rice fields

We train models by analyzing extensive data on rice crops under various salinity conditions. This process involves identifying patterns and features that help in accurately classifying crops, ensuring the model becomes increasingly accurate over time

This project is designed for agricultural researchers, farmers, and policymakers seeking to enhance crop management. It offers valuable insights for those focused on improving rice crop resilience to salinity stress and optimizing agricultural productivity
