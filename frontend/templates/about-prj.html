About the Project

Our major project focuses on predicting rice salinity levels using deep learning techniques. Salinity stress is a critical factor affecting rice crops, and accurate classification of rice varieties based on their tolerance levels can help improve agricultural practices. By analyzing images of rice plants, we aim to categorize them into five distinct classes: Highly Tolerant, Tolerant, Moderately Tolerant, Sensitive, and Highly Sensitive. We are leveraging powerful models like InceptionV3, VGG16, EfficientNet and ResNet to analyze visual data, applying ensemble learningfor more accurate predictions. This project involves extensive data preprocessing, model fine-tuning, and evaluation using key performance metrics such as accuracy and precision. Our goal is to provide a reliable solution that can aid farmers and agricultural scientists in identifying optimal rice varieties
